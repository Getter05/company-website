Why a Website Matters

Your customers are already looking online.

A website gives customers a place to learn who you are, what you offer, where you serve, and how to contact you. For many small businesses, a clean website can make the business look more established and easier to trust before the customer ever calls.

Of Americans are online

Source: DataReportal, Digital 2025

Of consumers read online reviews

97% of consumers read reviews for local businesses before making a decision. A website with a reviews section helps you build credibility.

Source: BrightLocal, 2026

72%

Of businesses have a website

72% of businesses had a website in 2025. If your competitors are online and you're not, customers will likely choose them first.
